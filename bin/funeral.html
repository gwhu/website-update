Funeral Singer Eileen Coyle

Funeral singer Eileen is a caring and understanding person, with a huge amount of experience. She will handle your mass with respect and sensitivity. She will guide you through the church or crematorium ceremony and help you chose suitable requiem hymns or contempory songs that mean something to you and your loved one. Eileen is based in Dublin but travels all over Ireland.

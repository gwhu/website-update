Sample Packages

Eileen works with only the best instrumentalists whom all have had years of training and experience performing at weddings as a church singer, at funerals and at other formal events. She has a list of popular duets and can arrange an extra Soprano or Tenor if needed. Additionally, Eileen is available with the 1940's themed Bugle Babes harmony trio for a bit of unique Vintage glamour and charm.

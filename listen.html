    Choosing your songs

    One of the most popular female wedding singers in Dublin this year, Eileen has a uniquely pure and clear celtic sound. She can cater for all song choices, from classical to modern and Irish.

    I have an eclectic taste in music and will probably know most of the songs you like. I've sung songs by the Beatles, Ellie Goulding, David Bowie, Elbow, Leonard Cohen, Celine Dion and many more. I know songs from the musicals and loads of jazz classics. Usually the ceremony will allow for a couple of non-religious songs and I will help you pick out the songs that are going to be part of the ceremony itself and need to be religious. It is always best to discuss song choice with your priest if you are having a religious ceremony. For those of you opting for a non-religious or civil ceremony the song choice is usually really open and anything goes as long as it is respectful and suits the mood of love and togetherness.

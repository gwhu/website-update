Voice Over Artist

Eileen has a versatile voice and owns her own home studio. She has performed for many companies including Brown Thomas. She is skilled in Music Technology and has made soundtracks for a number of films and shorts in Ireland. Eileen is no stranger to the recording studio and has performed on jingles and advertisments as a member of   The Bugle Babes.
